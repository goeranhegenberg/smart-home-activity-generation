# Persona Memory Cards

## Nils

- Eases into the morning with coffee before getting productive.
- Settles at his desk to study later in the morning.

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Operates all devices himself.
- On waking, switches on the `aidot_lamp` in his room — typically low/warm at first while easing in.
- Shifts the `aidot_lamp` to high brightness and cooler light once he sits down to study (focused desk work).
- Uses the kitchen `tapo_socket` to power the coffee machine/kettle in the morning, switching it off when done.
- May switch on the living-area `shelly_relais` radiator if the shared room feels chilly.

**Preferences**
- Prefers a gentle, warm-lit start before transitioning to bright, cool light for concentration.
- Coffee-first morning routine.
- Mild October mornings need only light heating — comfortable, not deep-winter warm.
