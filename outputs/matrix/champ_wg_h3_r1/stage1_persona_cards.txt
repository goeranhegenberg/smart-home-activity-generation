# Persona Memory Cards

## Nils
**Role:** Graduate student, works from home (no commute), shares the flat.

**Daily schedule (morning):**
- Wakes around 07:30.
- Eases into the day with coffee before getting going.
- Settles at his desk to study later in the morning.

**Smart-home habits:**
- Operates devices himself, room by room.
- On waking, turns on the `aidot_lamp` in his room — typically low/warm to ease in.
- Switches it to high (and cooler) once he sits down to focus on studying at his desk.
- Uses the kitchen `tapo_socket` to power the coffee machine/kettle for his morning coffee, then switches it off when done.
- May turn on the shared `tuya_lamp` and the `shelly_relais` radiator when passing through the living area on a grey morning.

**Preferences:**
- Slow, relaxed start; warm low light early, brighter cool light for focused study.
- Coffee is part of the wake-up routine.
- Only light heating needed this mild October morning.

## Jana
**Role:** Freelance designer, works from home, shares the flat.

**Daily schedule (morning):**
- Wakes around 08:00.
- Flexible morning: makes breakfast, no rush.
- Starts client work mid-morning at her small workspace.

**Smart-home habits:**
- Operates devices herself.
- On waking, turns on the `tapo_lamp` in her room — warm to start, brighter for getting going and for her workspace.
- Uses the kitchen `tapo_socket` for coffee/kettle during breakfast; switches it off afterwards.
- Shares the living-area `tuya_lamp` and `shelly_relais` radiator; comfortable turning the radiator off once the room feels warm enough.

**Preferences:**
- Relaxed, unhurried mornings overlapping loosely with Nils.
- Warm, comfortable lighting while easing in; adequate light for design work mid-morning.
- Mindful of shared devices and modest heating on this mild day.

**Shared notes:**
- Two flatmates, loosely overlapping routines; shared kitchen `tapo_socket` and living-area `tuya_lamp` / `shelly_relais` used by both.
