# Persona Memory Cards

## Alice — Work-From-Home Professional & Parent
**Daily schedule**
- 07:00 — Wakes up
- 07:00–08:00 — Helps prepare Charlie for school with Bob (getting dressed, breakfast)
- ~08:00–08:30 — Indoor exercise after the school drop-off
- 08:30 — Starts work at her desk in the Living Area
- Daytime — Works from home at her desk

**Smart-home habits**
- Uses the `aidot_lamp` (Bedroom) on waking; prefers it on warm, dim while Bob is still around in the dark.
- Relies on the `tuya_lamp` (Living Area) for her desk work — sets brightness `high` for focused tasks.
- Keeps the `shelly_relais` radiator (Living Area) on through the cold morning since she stays home; switches off when the room is warm enough.

**Preferences**
- Bright, well-lit workspace during working hours.
- Comfortable, heated living area since she's home all day.
- Warm light for waking and family time; brighter light for focus.

## Bob — 9-to-5 Office Worker & Parent
**Daily schedule**
- 06:00 — Wakes up early, gets ready quickly
- ~06:15 — Eats breakfast
- 06:45 — Helps Charlie get up
- 07:15 — Breakfast with Charlie
- 08:30 — Leaves with Charlie for commute + school drop-off

**Smart-home habits**
- Switches on `aidot_lamp` (Bedroom) early; keeps brightness considerate of sleeping Alice (often `low`/`warm`) before fully waking the room.
- Turns on `tapo_lamp` (Charlie's Room) to wake Charlie gently at 06:45.
- Powers the `tapo_socket` (Kitchen) for kettle/coffee at breakfast; switches it off when done.
- May turn on `shelly_relais` radiator and `tuya_lamp` in the Living Area for the morning routine.

**Preferences**
- Fast, efficient morning with minimal fuss.
- Switches devices off on the way out when the apartment empties (energy-conscious).
- Warm light early; doesn't want to disturb Alice before 07:00.

## Charlie — 8-year-old Primary School Child
**Daily schedule**
- 06:45 — Wakes up (needs bedroom lamp on to get up in the dark)
- 07:15 — Breakfast with Bob
- Morning — Gets dressed with Alice's help
- 08:30 — Leaves with Bob for school

**Smart-home habits**
- Cannot operate devices autonomously.
- Frequently triggers actions *through the adults* — asks for more light, a warmer room, or the TV.
- Depends on the `tapo_lamp` (Charlie's Room) being switched on by a parent to wake and dress.

**Preferences**
- Needs adequate light to wake and get dressed on dark winter mornings.
- Likes a warm, cozy room.
- Comfort-driven requests passed to Alice or Bob.
