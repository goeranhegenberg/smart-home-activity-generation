# Persona Memory Cards

## Lena (38, hospital nurse — rotating shifts)

**Daily schedule (varies strongly by day):**
- **Mon/Tue (early):** leaves ~05:40, home ~14:30
- **Wed (day off):** at home — errands, rest, daytime leisure
- **Thu/Fri (late):** leaves ~13:00, home ~22:15
- **Sat (night):** sleeps in, afternoon nap, leaves ~20:30, works overnight
- **Sun:** returns ~07:15, sleeps through much of morning/early afternoon

**Smart-home habits:**
- Operates devices herself
- On early shifts: uses `bedroom_lamp` (cool/high) to wake in the dark, brief `bathroom_lamp` and `hallway_lamp` when leaving
- Turns on `heating` on cold mornings/evenings she is home; off when leaving
- May run `robot_vacuum` on her Wednesday day off
- Uses `bedroom_lamp` (warm/low) to wind down before sleep

**Preferences:**
- Energy-conscious: switches lights off when leaving a room
- Cool/bright light when alert (getting up), warm/dim when relaxing
- Schedule is the least predictable in the household — anchor to the weekly rota
