# Persona Memory Cards

## Alice — Work-From-Home Professional & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 07:00 — Wakes up
- 07:00–08:00 — Helps prepare Charlie for school with Bob
- ~08:30 — School drop-off handled by Bob; Alice exercises indoors
- 08:30 — Settles at her desk in the living area to start work
- Spends most of the day in the Living Area work corner

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Relies on the `aidot_lamp` (bedroom) to start the morning in the dark.
- Manages the Living Area for both comfort and work: `tuya_lamp` for desk illumination, `shelly_relais` to warm the room before settling in.
- Likely turns the radiator on early so the living area is warm by work-start.

**Preferences**
- Prefers bright, clear light for focused work (`tuya_lamp` high).
- Values a warm, heated living space during winter mornings.
- Comfortable, cozy lighting for waking; brighter light once active.

**Preferences**

- Considerate of sleeping Alice — dim, warm light early (`aidot_lamp` low/warm).

- 06:45 — Wakes up (needs bedroom lamp on to rise in the dark)
- 07:15 — Eats breakfast with Bob
- Morning — Gets dressed with Alice's help
- 08:30 — Leaves with Bob for school
