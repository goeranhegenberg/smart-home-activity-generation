# Persona Memory Cards

## Alice — Work-From-Home Professional & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 07:00 — Wakes up
- 07:00–08:30 — Prepares Charlie for school with Bob; helps Charlie get dressed
- ~08:30 — School drop-off handled by Bob; Alice exercises indoors
- 08:30 onward — Starts work at her desk in the Living Area

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Spends most of the day in the Living Area, so she manages the `tuya_lamp` (desk/ceiling light) and `shelly_relais` (radiator) there.
- Likely turns on the living-area radiator early to warm the shared space on chilly mornings.
- Switches the `tuya_lamp` to **high** brightness for focused work at her desk.

**Preferences**
- Values a warm, well-lit work corner during dark winter mornings.
- Comfort-conscious about room temperature since she's home all day — keeps the radiator running while present, off when the room is warm enough or empty.
- Bedroom `aidot_lamp`: prefers a gentler start than Bob (wakes later at 07:00).

## Bob — 9-to-5 Office Worker & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 06:00 — Wakes up early, gets ready quickly
- ~06:15–06:45 — Eats breakfast
- 06:45 — Helps Charlie get up
- 07:15 — Breakfast with Charlie
- 08:30 — Leaves with Charlie for commute + school drop-off

**Smart-Home Habits**
- First one up: switches on the bedroom `aidot_lamp` in the dark, likely **low/warm** to avoid disturbing sleeping Alice.
- Turns on Charlie's `tapo_lamp` at 06:45 to wake him gently.
- Operates the kitchen `tapo_socket` (kettle/coffee machine) for morning breakfast drinks.
- On the way out, helps switch off devices no longer needed.

**Preferences**
- Efficient, fast morning routine.
- Mindful of not waking Alice — favors dim, warm light early in the bedroom.
- Practical: powers kitchen appliance for breakfast, cuts it off once done.

## Charlie — 8-Year-Old Primary School Child

**Daily Schedule**
- 06:45 — Wakes up (needs bedroom lamp on to get up in the dark)
- 07:15 — Eats breakfast with Bob
- After breakfast — Gets dressed with Alice's help
- 08:30 — Leaves with Bob for school

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Cannot operate devices autonomously.
- Frequently triggers actions *via the adults* — asking for more light, a warmer room, or the TV.
- Relies on the `tapo_lamp` in his room being switched on to wake and dress.

**Preferences**
- Needs adequate light to get up and dressed on dark winter mornings — `tapo_lamp` at usable brightness.
- Likes warmth and comfort; may prompt requests to turn on the `shelly_relais` radiator in the living area.
- Depends entirely on Alice and Bob to act on his comfort requests.
