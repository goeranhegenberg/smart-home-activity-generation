# Persona Memory Card — Nils

**Role:** Graduate student, works from home (no commute), shares the flat with Jana.

**Daily Schedule (morning):**
- Wakes around 07:30, no rush.
- Eases into the day with coffee.
- Studies at his desk later in the morning.

**Smart-Home Habits:**
- Operates all devices himself.
- On waking, turns on the `aidot_lamp` in his room — starts dim/warm to ease into the morning.
- Switches the `aidot_lamp` to brighter, cooler light when settling in to study at his desk.
- Uses the kitchen `tapo_socket` to power the coffee machine/kettle for his morning coffee; switches it off when done.
- May turn on the `shelly_relais` radiator in the shared living area to take the chill off on grey mornings.

**Preferences:**
- Warm, low light for the slow start; cool, high brightness for focused study.
- Mild October mornings need only light heating — radiator on briefly, off once comfortable.
- Relaxed, gradual morning routine.

# Persona Memory Card — Jana

**Role:** Freelance designer, works from home, shares the flat with Nils.

**Daily Schedule (morning):**
- Wakes around 08:00, flexible start.
- Makes breakfast.
- Begins client work mid-morning.

**Smart-Home Habits:**
- Operates all devices herself.
- On waking, turns on the `tapo_lamp` in her room to light up and wake.
- Uses the kitchen `tapo_socket` for coffee/breakfast appliance; turns it off when finished.
- Turns on the shared-area `tuya_lamp` when moving through the living area for breakfast.
- May adjust or switch off the `shelly_relais` radiator once the living area feels comfortable.

**Preferences:**
- Bright light to wake up and for design work clarity.
- Comfortable but not overheated shared space — mindful of the mild October weather.
- Loosely overlapping routine with Nils; shares kitchen and living-area devices cooperatively.

---

*Note: Devices are room-bound — each resident controls only the devices in the room they currently occupy (or pass through). Shared devices (`tuya_lamp`, `shelly_relais`, `tapo_socket`) are used cooperatively.*
