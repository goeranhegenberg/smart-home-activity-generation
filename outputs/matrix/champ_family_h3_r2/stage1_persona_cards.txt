# Persona Memory Cards

## Alice — Work-From-Home Professional & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 07:00 — Wakes up
- 07:00–08:30 — Prepares Charlie for school with Bob; helps him get dressed
- ~08:30 — School drop-off handled by Bob; Alice stays home
- After drop-off — Indoor exercise
- 08:30 onward — Works at her desk in the Living Area

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Uses the bedroom `aidot_lamp` when waking and dressing in the dark.
- Helps light Charlie's room (`tapo_lamp`) while assisting him.
- Relies on the Living Area `tuya_lamp` for her desk and workspace.
- Turns on the `shelly_relais` radiator to warm the Living Area before settling in to work.
- May keep the `tapo_socket` (kettle/coffee) running for morning drinks.

**Preferences**
- Wants the Living Area warm and well-lit for focused work — bright `tuya_lamp`, radiator on.
- Prefers cool, bright light for alertness during work hours.
- Comfort-conscious on chilly winter mornings; keeps the home heated while present.
