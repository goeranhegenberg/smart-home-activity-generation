# Persona Memory Card

## Mara

**Profile:** Single professional, lives alone in a compact two-room apartment in Germany. Operates all devices herself. Winter mornings are dark and chilly, so the routine is short, efficient, and warmth-focused.

### Daily Schedule (Workday Morning)
- **06:30** — Wakes up in the dark bedroom.
- **06:30–07:00** — Gets up and dresses; needs bright light against the winter dark.
- **07:00–07:45** — Heats the living area, prepares a breakfast drink, has a quick breakfast.
- **By 08:00** — Leaves the apartment for her commute; all non-essential devices switched off.

### Smart-Home Habits
- **Bedroom (aidot_lamp):** First light of the day. Turns it on bright and cool to wake up alert while dressing; switches off (~06:48) when leaving the bedroom.
- **Living area heating (shelly_relais):** Turns the radiator on early (~06:52) to warm the cold room; switches off (~07:43) when leaving for work.
- **Kitchen (tapo_socket):** Powers the coffee machine briefly for the morning drink; switches off once ready (~07:01).
- **Living lights:** Uses the tuya_lamp ceiling light at high brightness first for general illumination, then switches to the tapo_lamp corner lamp (low, warm) for a cozy breakfast and turns off the harsh ceiling light.
- **Departure routine:** Ensures all lights, socket, and heater are off when leaving by 08:00.

### Preferences
- **Brightness:** `high` while waking/dressing and for general illumination; switches to `low` for a cozy breakfast.
- **Light temperature:** `cool` first thing to wake up alert; `warm` for the relaxed breakfast moment.
- **Efficiency & energy-consciousness:** Single occupant with an early commute — minimizes wasted energy, turns everything off when nobody is home.
- **Warmth first:** Prioritizes heating the chilly apartment before the day's routine begins.
