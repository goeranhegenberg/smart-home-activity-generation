# Persona Memory Cards

## Alice — Work-From-Home Professional & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 07:00 — Wakes up
- 07:00–08:30 — Prepares Charlie for school with Bob; helps him get dressed
- ~08:30 — School drop-off handled by Bob; Alice exercises indoors
- 08:30 onward — Starts working at her desk in the Living Area

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Relies on the `aidot_lamp` (bedroom) when getting up in the dark.
- Uses the `tuya_lamp` (living area) over her work corner — bright illumination for focused work.
- Keeps the `shelly_relais` (living-area radiator) running on chilly mornings, especially since she stays home all day.
- May use the `tapo_socket` (kitchen) for coffee before settling into work.

**Preferences**
- Bright, well-lit work environment (high brightness, cool light where available).
- Warm, comfortable living area for long work hours.
- Energy-aware but prioritizes comfort since she occupies the flat all day.

## Bob — 9-to-5 Office Worker & Parent

**Daily Schedule**
- 06:00 — Wakes up early, gets ready quickly
- ~06:30 — Eats breakfast
- 06:45 — Helps Charlie get up
- 07:15 — Breakfast with Charlie
- 08:30 — Leaves with Charlie for commute + school drop-off

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Briefly uses `aidot_lamp` (bedroom) when waking — keeps it dim/warm to avoid disturbing Alice.
- Turns on `tapo_lamp` (Charlie's room) to wake Charlie gently at 06:45.
- Operates the `tapo_socket` (kitchen) for kettle/coffee at breakfast.
- Switches on `shelly_relais` early to warm the living area before the family gathers.
- Passes through Hallway on departure; mindful of switching off devices no longer needed.

**Preferences**
- Fast, efficient morning routine.
- Warm, cozy lighting early (low disturbance to sleeping partner).
- Practical: warms the apartment early, powers breakfast appliances.

## Charlie — 8-Year-Old Primary School Child

**Daily Schedule**
- 06:45 — Wakes up (needs bedroom lamp on to rise in the dark)
- 07:15 — Breakfast with Bob
- After breakfast — Gets dressed with Alice's help
- 08:30 — Leaves with Bob for school

**Smart-Home Habits**
- Cannot operate devices autonomously.
- Triggers actions *via the adults*: asks for the `tapo_lamp` (his room) to be switched on to wake and dress, requests more light, a warmer room, or the TV.
- Depends on others for the `tapo_lamp`, `tuya_lamp`, `shelly_relais`, etc.

**Preferences**
- Needs `tapo_lamp` on (bright enough to get dressed) during dark mornings.
- Likes warmth and comfort — prompts adults to turn up heating/lighting.
- Comfort-seeking; enjoys shared time (e.g. TV) in the living area.
