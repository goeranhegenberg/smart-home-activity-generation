# Persona Memory Card

## Mara

**Profile:** Single professional, lives alone in a compact two-room apartment in Germany. Works a standard 9-to-5 office job. Operates all devices herself.

---

### Daily Schedule (Weekday Morning)
- **06:30** — Wakes up in a dark, chilly bedroom.
- **06:30–07:00** — Gets up, dresses, freshens up.
- **07:00–07:45** — Quick breakfast and morning drink in kitchen/living area.
- **08:00** — Leaves the apartment for her commute via the hallway.

---

### Smart-Home Habits
- **Wake-up (Bedroom):** Turns on the `aidot_lamp` first thing — needs real light on dark winter mornings to wake and dress.
- **Heating (Living/Work Area):** Switches on `shelly_relais` (electric radiator) early to warm the cold room before the day starts; turns it off when leaving — never heats an empty apartment.
- **Breakfast (Kitchen):** Powers the `tapo_socket` (kettle/coffee machine) to prepare her morning drink, then cuts power once done.
- **Living area lighting:** Uses `tuya_lamp` (main ceiling) for general illumination and/or `tapo_lamp` for the reading corner before leaving.
- **Departure:** Shuts everything off on the way out — efficient, no devices left running.

---

### Preferences
- **Light temperature:** Favors `warm` light for the cozy early morning; may switch to `cool` when she wants to feel alert.
- **Brightness:** Prefers `high` brightness for getting dressed and focused morning tasks; `low` only for ambient/wind-down moments.
- **Efficiency-minded:** Single occupant with a short routine — values quick, minimal interactions and turning devices off when not needed (energy- and cost-conscious).
- **Comfort priority:** Wants the apartment warm and well-lit before facing a cold, dark commute.
