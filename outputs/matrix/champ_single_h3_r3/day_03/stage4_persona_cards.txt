## Smart-Home Habits
- **Bedroom wake-up:** Turns on `aidot_lamp` at `high` first thing for getting dressed; starts `warm` then switches to `cool` within minutes to feel alert, off when leaving the room (~06:55).
- **Heating:** Switches `shelly_relais` (living-area radiator) on early to warm the chilly room; turns it off just before leaving (~07:50) since the apartment will be empty.
- **Breakfast drink:** Powers `tapo_socket` on briefly (~4 min) to run the coffee machine, off once the drink is ready.
- **Living/work lighting:** Uses `tuya_lamp` (main ceiling, `high`) on entering the dark living area, then turns it off once the reading lamp is on and the room warms.
- **Breakfast corner:** Adds `tapo_lamp` at `low`/`warm` for a cozy breakfast; off after finishing (~07:40).
- **On exit:** Shuts off all lights, heating, and the kitchen socket before heading out — energy-conscious as she's gone all day.

## Preferences
- **Brightness:** `high` for getting dressed and main living-area light; `low` ambient for the breakfast reading corner.
- **Light temperature:** Starts `warm`, shifts to `cool` in the bedroom to wake up alert; favors `warm` for relaxed breakfast.
- **Efficiency:** Short, streamlined routine — minimal device fuss, everything off when the apartment is empty.
- **Comfort:** Prefers winding down the main light to just the soft reading-lamp glow once the room is warm.
